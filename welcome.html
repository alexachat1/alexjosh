So what is Valorant?

Valorant, is a 5 person tactile shooter, that utilises teamwork to beat an opposing team.

There is a number of different environments to play on, commonly known as maps.

There are multiple characters to pick and choose, known as aganets, and they all have their own little twists to help you pick your perfect playstyle.

Each of these agents fulfil different roles to benefit a team. These roles consist of: Duelist, Initiator, Controller and Sentinel.

Duelist

The duelist class, utilises agent abilities to help enter a spike site, and create space for the rest of your team.

The duelist class, currently has 6 agents, who go by the names of: Reyna, Jett, Raze, Pheonix, Yoru and Neon

Reyna is an agent obtained from Mexico who has 2 blinds (C), 2 heals (E) or dismisses (Q), and her ultimte (X) is empress, which allows the user to gain health and dismiss at the same time, while having increase rate of fire, and reload speed.

Jett is a South Koreon who can contorl the wind with 2 smokes (C), a dash (E), 2 updrafts (Q) and her ultimate (X) is knives, which gives the user 5 knives to try eliminate opponents, and her knives refresh with every succesful elimination.

Raze comes from the heart of Brazil who can make mexchanical explosives, who uses a boom bot (C), a granade (E), 2 satchels (Q), and her ultimate is a rocket laucher, which she can fire to create large damage to opponents.

Pheonix is an agent hired from England, who has the ability to manipulate fire, he has a fire wall (C), a fire molly (E), 2 blinds (Q) and his ultimate (X) allows him to have a second chance at life, with resurection.

Yoru is a Japense agent who uses dimensional drift to his advantage, he has a fake decoy (C), 2 teleports, 1 being real and 1 being fake (E), 2 blinds (Q), and his ultimate is called dimensional drift, that allows him to become invisible to enemies, while being able to gain information on their wherabouts.

Neon is from the philophines, who uses energy surges to give herself a energy wall (C), 2 stuns (E) a sprint (Q), while active she can slide (Right Click) and her ultimate is a laser beam, that helps to eliminate enemies usin her massive energy surge.

Most duelists refresh their E with 2 eliminations, hoever Neon only requires 1, and Reyna cannot refresh her E/Q.

Initiator

Initators are viable to a team, as they allow fights to begin, with an advantage to their own team.

Initator utility tends to rely on imformation gain, to help teams understand how an opposing team may have set up their defence, or offence.

There are currently five Initators in Valorant, and they go by the names of: Breach, Fade, Sova, Skye and Kayo.

Breach is a swedish mechanic, that gave himself robotic arms to perform certian tasks, these tasks compiles of an aftershock (C) which can damage enimes through solid walls, 2 blinds (Q), a stun (E) and his ultimate (X) is a massive stun which can cover large grounds.

Fade is the newest addition to Valorant, who originate from Turkey, her abilities come from scaring others, such that she has 2 prowlers (C) that can find people,

Sova is a russian guinesius who speacilises in finding the locations of his enemies, he has a drone (C), 2 shock darts (Q), a recon dart (E), and his ultimate (X), allows his to burst large amounts of damage across the map, to try and eliminate opponents.

Skye is an aussie born, fascinated with animals, and her abilities show it, begining with her (C), she can heal team mates within a certain radius, she has a dog (Q) that can find eneimies, 2 birds that act as blinds (E) and alerts the user if someone was succesively blinded, and her ultimate can track down eneimies across the map.

Kayo is a robot created to spread order, and to supress the utility of others, if needed, he has a molly (C) which harms enemies with strong impulses, 2 blinds (Q), a supression knife (E) that can be thrown to either supress enemies within a certain radius, or just to gian imformation, and his ultimate (X) supresses all enemies abilities within a radius for 20 seconds, and if the user were to be taken out, he can be revived for a certain period of time.

Initiators refresh their (E) after a certain period of time.
